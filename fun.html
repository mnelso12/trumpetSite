The fam has fun

we're pretty awesome, and we know it

The Notre Dame Band includes students from accross the United States and around the world. The Notre Dame Victory March was first performed in 1908. The March, composed by John and Michael Shea (Notre Dame students), has become one of the most recognized, and copied college fight songs ever. In a recent evaluation of the nation's greatest fight songs, USA Today ranked Notre Dame's as the Best College Fight Song in existence! In a recent Harris Poll, the Notre Dame football team was also ranked as the most recognizable, and revered college football team.



Trumpet Football



Trumpets have dominated the band football league every year since its start in 1993. Our biggest rivals are the saxes and trombones - but never fear, trumpets always win. Long live Schmed and his fertile athleticism.





Thistleberry Farm

Service Trip



Every Fall Break we have a band service trip, one of many service opportunities as band members. We do everything - help with ESL classes, clean yards, organize donations, build houses, clean kitchens, volunteer at homeless shelters, prepare food, and play for lots and lots of kiddos.
