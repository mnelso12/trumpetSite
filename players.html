Become a Trumpet

In order to become a part of one of the country's greatest, and most recognizable bands, you must first go through a training period known as Band Camp. Band Camp starts about 5 days before the first day of classes here on campus, and students participating in band camp are allowed to move into the dorms earlier than the other students.

Trying out for the band consists of two separate auditions:

1. Playing Audition
You play some required music which the Band mails to you during the summer, as well as another piece that you think demonstrates your musical ability. This is done as a solo in front of two directors present.

2. Marching Audition
On the final day of camp, this audition demonstrates auditionees' ability to learn simple marching drills and perform them accurately. The marching audition is performed in groups of 16 or 20 with the entire band watching and playing the music.

As a first-time auditionee, you may be contacted by a Band Ambassador. These friendly, dedicated band members work very hard to recruit the most promising future band members from all over the country (and the world!).

First-time auditionees are also contacted by their own personal Band Buddy. Your band buddy is an experienced trumpet player whose job is to welcome you to the trumpet family and answer any questions you may have.

pro tip: attitude matters as much as marching and playing ability

Each year, members returning to the band must go through the marching audition again, and the possibility does exist that they could be cut from the band if there are freshmen that deserve the honor more.

Anyone who is interested in becoming a band member should contact the Band office, and ask to be put on the mailing list. Someone will arrange for you to move into your dorm early and get you signed up to participate in band camp. The office can be reached by phone at (574) 631-7136, e-mail at ndband@nd.edu, or by snail mail at University Bands, PO box 1032, Notre Dame, IN 46556. For more information about the Notre Dame Band and the audition process, visit the Official Notre Dame Band web site http://www.ndband.com.

If you are a trumpet player planning on applying to Notre Dame, e-mail us at trumpets@nd.edu, and we will fill you in on important information regarding your tryouts!
